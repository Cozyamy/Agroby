    Our Story

    TuteeReelz was brought to life by a team of dynamic individuals working under ALX. After weeks of research, brainstorming and late night work, our team is excited to present our finished project.

    We all put in so much to give you an awesome streaming platform for short movies. This application is designed to allow you express and share your experiences through brief 30 minute videos.

    We are proud to give college students and short film enthusiasts a smooth working interface to help you all achieve your goals.

    Our mission

    As a team of hardworking developers, we are committed to our cause, which is providing our users with a hitch free enjoyable video interactive experience while using this app.

    We are commited to empowering young creative minds in impacting positively to the society through video streaming

    Our Goals

    Our aim is to give our users a safe space for positive video experession and interaction in our Website.

    We want users to enjoy a video environment where they can learn and contribute to different topics.

    We aim to provide an inclusive environment for our users focused on showcasing their videos in the best ways possible.

    TuteeReelz is a new product which enables college students share, download and comment on short videos.
